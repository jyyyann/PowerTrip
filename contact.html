Power up your fleet

Power Trip optimises the use of EVs across fleets. Our tools help companies easily transition their fleets to electric and plan the charging infrastructure they need to run them. Once EVs are in the fleet, Power Trip optimises booking, planning, driving, and EV charging with a suite of interactive, engaging solutions. When your EVs are ready to move on, Power Trip optimises their resale value and lower their environmental impact through advanced product stewardship integrations.
