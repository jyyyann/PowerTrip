Software solutions to make electric vehicles easy.

Power Trip optimises the use of EVs across fleets. Our tools help companies transition their fleets to electric and plan the charging infrastructure they need to run them. Once EVs are in the fleet, Power Trip enables vehicle booking, route planning and charger booking to optimise their use. We also offer tools to encourage safer and more efficient driving which saves money and improves safety across the fleet.

POWER TRIP SOLUTIONS


Power Trip uses vehicle, road and terrain data to estimate vehicle energy needs and create a step-by-step travel and charging itinerary.



Our Game Plan product provides rich data to customers on their vehicle use. Using this data, Power Trip Game Plan makes recommendations about which fleet vehicles can transition to electric and where those vehicles will need charging infrastructure installed for the most efficient driving and charging experience.
