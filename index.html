Software solutions to make
fleet electrification easy.

Power Trip optimises the use of EVs across fleets. Our tools help companies transition their fleets to electric and plan the charging infrastructure they need to run them. Once EVs are in the fleet, Power Trip enables vehicle booking, route planning and charger booking to optimise their use. We also offer tools to encourage safer and more efficient driving which saves money and improves safety across the fleet.

FLEET ELECTRIFICATION SOLUTIONS


We predict energy use, find charging stops and calculate optimal routes for electric vehicles. Connect Power Trip with your vehicles and charging stations to create powerful solutions for your fleet.



GAME PLAN


  Assess your existing vehicles


Link your telematics data to Power Trip's plug-and-play EV assessment and transition planner to create your EV strategy. Create heat maps and graphs showing how your existing fleet is used. Map this against our energy predictions to create recommendations for suitable EVs.



  Ongoing EV rollout strategy


Program your EV policies in to Power Trip to create regular, evolving updates on the feasibility of your EV replacement options. Look at your ongoing fleet data rather than relying on a one-off report.



  What charging infrastructure do you need?


Create a data-driven charging strategy to support your drivers and optimisze the rollout and utilisation of your charging assets. Use our heat maps to understand visually where you need to have chargers available for your drivers. Determine the right mix of public, private and home chargers for your fleet.


OPTIMISER


  Educate your drivers


Once you have EVs in your fleet, Bbring staff up top speed quicky with Power Trip's interactive, plug-and-play EV route planner and charging infrastructure map. Let your drivers experiment with different trips, routes and vehicles to create the best itinerary possible.



  Charging optimisation


Reduce the time spent charging while your staff are on the road with optimised routes from the Power Trip route planner. Link charging stops to meetings and other times vehicles are not in use to reduce waiting time.



  Productivity optimisation


Include your calendar appointments in your trip plans to find the minimum departure charge your drivers need to achieve a productive day.


DRIVER


  Power Trip fleet app


Incentivise safe, fuel efficient and low emissions driving and reward low-emissions transport choices with the Power Trip fleet app. Monitor speeding, harsh cornering and braking and other driving behaviour that is unsafe or inefficient.



  Engage drivers


Engage and reward drivers with scores that grow when they drive safely, efficiently and make low emissions choices.



  Data-driven feedback


Use your telematics data to give drivers the insights and feedback they need to improve their driving and get rewarded on the scoreboard.


NETWORK


  Power Trip charger booking


Make your charging stations bookable with the Power Trip charger booking system to encourage better utilisation of your assets.



  Manage internal schedules


Create staff schedules and permissions to manage charging times during work hours and ensure everyone gets the charge they need.



  Create a private network


Increase your charger utilisation and access a wider, private network of charging stations by sharing your chargers with other businesses.


STEWARD


  Power Trip battery tracker


Electric cars are two products in one, the battery and the vehicle. Track and maintain the health of your battery with Power Trip.



  Maintain battery health


Address behaviours that impact on battery health and capacity to maintain your assets resale value and preserve its driving range.



  Product stewardship


Reduce the future impact of your assets by participating in New Zealand's up-coming battery stewardship program.
