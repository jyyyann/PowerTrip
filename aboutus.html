Making EVs Easy

We provide tools and insights that take the stress out of managing your new electric vehicle fleet.

Who we are



We’re a bunch of friendly, climate-conscious Kiwis from New Zealand, a land full of winding hills, capricious weather and a diverse assortment of road types that can make long trips in electric cars an… exciting… experience! In fact, the idea for Power Trip was sparked when Power Trip’s founder, Richard, relied on the car’s in-built range guesser on his first EV trip and ended up crawling home with 4% left on the battery. The future is electric, and we’ve created Power Trip to do the maths, so that you can relax and focus on your journey.






Assess your existing vehicles



At its core Power Trip predicts EV energy use. We then apply these predictions in unique ways to help you make the most of your EV fleet.

  We tell you what your fleet’s driving would have looked like, if it had been done with a specific EV and where you’d have needed to charge.

  We manage EV driver education, charging and driving plans and help your drivers minimise any down-time for EV charging.

  We analyse, improve and reward safe, energy efficient driving styles. And we add intelligent management to your new energy assets, batteries and charging stations.

There’s plenty more under the hood, talk to us today to find an easy EV solution for you.



Power up your fleet

Power Trip optimises the use of EVs across fleets. Our tools help companies easily transition their fleets to electric and plan the charging infrastructure they need to run them. Once EVs are in the fleet, Power Trip optimises booking, planning, driving, and EV charging with a suite of interactive, engaging solutions. When your EVs are ready to move on, Power Trip optimises their resale value and lower their environmental impact through advanced product stewardship integrations.
